NOAA, Ocean Explorer | Lophelia II 2009 | Deep Wrecks

NOAA Ocean Explorer page on the Deep Wrecks portion of the Lophelia II 2009 expedition.  This is the intro before the mission actually took place – to explore 5 historic deepwater shipwrecks discovered during oil &; gas surveys.  Long but good overview and some good general intro to this kind of marine archaeology, nice photos – can make larger if able to click on them.  Also if can click on links, there\'s a cool QT video – a “3D” of one of the wrecks created by “draping” B/W photomosaic over bathymetry.  Would also make sense to include at least some of the log pages – log of day\'s exploration of each individual ship.  Long but interesting.All are explored w/Jason ROV.  Best would be 7,400 ft wreck (could link to environment), Green Lantern (could link to oil industry), and/or Viosca Knoll.
